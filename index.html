I love reading because the books take me to such different worlds with their own rules, languages, and magical powers.
Reading allows me to learn new words and learn writing skills. Reading expands my imagination and inspires me to write my own stories.


I would reccomend the series the Lunar Chronicles by Marissa Meyer. These books are my favorite books ever! These books are a mix between Fantasy & Sci-Fi.
This series is about a cyborg girl named Cinder who lives in New Beijing, this book takes place in the future! She is the best Mechanic in all of New Beijing.
One day, she meets Prince Kai of New Beijing, and her life changes. She finds out that she has hidden powers and more secrets from her past. Over the next
few books, she gathers allies to defeat her main enemy, Queen Levana of Luna. (Luna is a magical place on the moon). Read the books to find out what happens next!
